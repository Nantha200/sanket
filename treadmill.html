Treadmill (TMT) Test


Sanket Diagnostics offers advanced Treadmill Test (TMT) services to assess the heart's performance under physical stress. This non-invasive test helps detect signs of coronary artery disease (CAD), irregular heart rhythms, and overall cardiovascular health.


Our TMT procedures are conducted by skilled technicians under the supervision of experienced cardiologists, ensuring patient safety, accurate monitoring, and reliable interpretation of results.


When is TMT Recommended?


TMT is useful for individuals experiencing symptoms such as chest pain, shortness of breath, or those with risk factors for heart disease.


  • Evaluation of chest pain or discomfort
  • Assessment of cardiac fitness in athletes
  • Post-cardiac treatment or surgery monitoring
  • Detection of exercise-induced arrhythmias
  • Preventive screening for heart disease risk

During the TMT procedure, you will walk on a treadmill while your heart rate, blood pressure, and ECG are continuously monitored. This allows our cardiology team to evaluate how well your heart performs under stress and identify any underlying issues.
