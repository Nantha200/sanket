Some Story

30 Years Of Experience

Sanket Diagnostics Ltd is Zambia’s leader in advanced diagnostic services. They are NHIMA accredited and supported by a world-class multi-disciplinary medical team.

Sanket Diagnostics Centre was established in 1992 with the vision to serve the community with world-class professionals and technology. Sanket’s global hub is in Gujarat, India, which supports Sanket Zambia with specialty & sub-specialty in pathology. In our journey of 30 years, we have successfully served more than 20 million patients. Our patient-centred pathology services include more than 2000 tests in the disciplines of Hematology, Biochemistry, Microbiology, Serology, Histopathology, and Immunoassays as well as in the area of infectious diseases.

Sanket India started its way to Zambia in April 2012, with the opening of its affiliate company Sanket Diagnostics (Z) in Lusaka, Zambia. Hospitals in Zambia, both government and private sector, work hand in hand with Sanket. Zambia’s leading independent laboratory with Seventeen (17) operations across Zambia, as well as a partner in leading imaging centres in the country, Sanket-Sunol. Sanket presently leads a team of 250+ professionals and actively fosters research initiatives by covering 60% of the expenses associated with case studies conducted by medical residents

FOUNDER & DIRECTOR

MR. SALIM ADAM MUSA PATEL

In 2012, Mr. Salim Patel embarked on his transformative journey in Zambia, dedicating the past 11 years to establishing over 10 cutting-edge laboratories across the nation. Beyond his entrepreneurial pursuits, Mr. Patel is a Philanthropist with a focus on the development of women and children in Zambia and has adopted orphanages. His commitment to social welfare extends to the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, where he and his dedicated staff emerged as a pillar of support for the entire nation.

Mr. Patel actively contributes to the professional development of healthcare practitioners. He plays a pivotal role in organizing and participating in major Continuing Medical Education (CME) events, fostering the enhancement of doctors' knowledge and technical expertise. Through his multifaceted contributions, Mr. Patel has become a beacon of positive change and progress in Zambia’s healthcare landscape.

OUR VISION

We’re all about delivering world-class diagnostics using more than 4000 tests in the medical lab with high-tech radiology departments that help Zambians to achieve the best life they can through good health. Every day, our team works to be Zambia’s most trusted multidisciplinary diagnostics services provider delivering expert services on behalf of the world’s leading Quality Control Consultancy, Randox. Quality control consulting to hospital and standalone laboratories across Zambia. As for Sanket’s own diagnostics services, we offer Pathology, Microbiology, Biochemistry, Histology, Genetics, Histopathology, IHC, Cold Biopsy, MRI, Echo And Doppler, as well as 64 Slice CT Scan, Digital X Ray, and Mammography. All Sanket services are provided by licensed professionals expert in advanced medical technologies and laboratory best practices.

OUR VALUES

Sanket’s Core Values were created by our team more than a decade ago and act as guiding principles for how we conduct ourselves as an organization. Our Core Values set the standard for professionalism with which we conduct ourselves in our day-to-day duties. Individually, our Core Values articulate our commitment to medical excellence. Collectively, they empower our people to deliver exceptional medical services to doctors, patients, insurance schemes like NHIMA as well as collaborator health systems.
Since their inception, Sanket’s Core Values have been our business’ unifying code of conduct.
