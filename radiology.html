Radiology


Sanket Diagnostics offers a comprehensive range of advanced Radiology Services designed to support accurate diagnosis and treatment planning. Our radiology department is equipped with state-of-the-art imaging technology and staffed by experienced radiologists to deliver high-quality, timely results.


From basic X-rays to high-resolution imaging, we provide a wide array of radiological investigations that help clinicians detect, monitor, and manage a variety of health conditions with precision and confidence.


Radiology Services Offered


We ensure patient safety and comfort while maintaining the highest standards of diagnostic accuracy.


  • Digital X-Ray (Chest, Abdomen, Spine, Joints, etc.)
  • Ultrasound & Doppler Scans
  • CT Scan (Contrast & Non-Contrast)
  • MRI Scans (Brain, Spine, Joints, and more)
  • Mammography & Bone Densitometry (DEXA)

Our imaging department integrates technology with expert interpretation, enabling faster diagnostics and comprehensive patient care. We follow strict quality assurance protocols and offer both printed and digital reports for convenient access.
